## Bio
Dr. Marcus J. Reinhardt is an Associate Professor specializing in distributed systems and cloud-native infrastructure. His research focuses on building scalable, resilient platforms for large-scale data processing, with interests in edge computing and system reliability. He collaborates with industry partners on real-world deployment of distributed architectures.
